FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents before registering their child for Mutahus Global school van service.

Mutahus Global currently covers SK Bertam Perdana, SMK Bertam Perdana, SMK Dato Kailan, KAFA Bertam Perdana, KAFA Masjid Abdullah Fahim and KAFA Paya Keladi.

Parents need to create an account, login to the parent dashboard, then click Register Child and fill in the child’s school, class, session, home address and pickup location.

Payment can be made manually through bank transfer or DuitNow. After payment, parents can upload the receipt from the Payment Proof page for admin review.

If there is a delay due to traffic, weather or route changes, parents can check announcements on the parent dashboard. Admin can also post delay notices.

Parents can use the WhatsApp button on the website to contact Mutahus Global directly for urgent questions, route updates or emergency matters.

New child registrations need to be reviewed by admin first. Admin will update the status to Accepted, Rejected or Active after checking the submitted information.
